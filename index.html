WHAT IS WAITING FOR YOU?

HONORS ELECTIVE CLASSES

With all of the AP and Honors classes offered in our school, these classes are a great way to keep your GPA up while still taking a stress-free elective course. Your class will continue to learn and grow together throughout the three-year program. 

WEB DESIGN FAMILY

Each year, you will move into the next course with the same students (and possibly the same teacher) you began your web design journey with! This allows you to make deeper connections with your classmates. 

CREATIVE FREEDOM

Web design is not only about coding on a computer screen; it is an art. If you have any artistic abilities, web design is a great way to expand your skills into a field that will open countless doors in your future.

COMPUTER LITERACY

It does not matter how much you know about computers, we will teach you everything you need to know! You will leave this program with knowledge and skills that every college/employer will see as an asset.
